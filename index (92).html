About Infinite Solutions

We are pioneers in automated parking systems, providing innovative solutions that maximize space utilization and enhance user convenience. With cutting-edge technology and a commitment to excellence, we transform parking challenges into seamless experiences. Our systems are designed for reliability, efficiency, and sustainability, making urban parking smarter and more accessible.

Based in Kalyan, Maharashtra, we have successfully completed over 5,000 parking spaces across India, serving 55+ satisfied clients. Our expertise lies not just in manufacturing but in creating solutions that meet the unique requirements of each project.

Our Consultant Network

We work with 15+ leading consultants across India through our exclusive white-label partnership program. Our consultants market and sell our automated parking systems under their own brand name, providing them with comprehensive support including technical expertise, installation services, and after-sales maintenance. This partnership model allows consultants to offer world-class parking solutions while we focus on manufacturing excellence and innovation. Together, we're revolutionizing parking infrastructure throughout the country.
